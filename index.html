FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

The AGRO VIKING ROVER is designed to tackle every common problem that a traditional drone/robot faces. It can easily navigate through difficult terrains while being more budget friendly than EVER.

The rover is equipped with a rechargeable battery pack, ensuring extended operational life. Additionally, it incorporates two independent subsystems, significantly reducing the likelihood of mechanical failures and enhancing overall reliability.

The rover is equipped with soil analyzers, a high-resolution camera and A robotic arm for soil sample collection.
